A community conference for data makers everywhere

Plans for csv,conf,v4 are underway. Dates and location for the 2019 conference will be announced soon.

In the meantime, you can revisit csv,conf,v1, csv,conf,v2 and csv,conf,v3 conference websites.

csv,conf,v4 is about bringing a diverse group together to discuss data topics, and features stories about data sharing and data analysis from science, journalism, government, and open source.

Building Community

We bring together data makers/doers/hackers from backgrounds like science, journalism, open government and the wider software industry to share knowledge and stories.

For those who love data

csv,conf is a non-profit community conference run by some folks who really love data and sharing knowledge. If you are as passionate about data and the application it has to society as we are, then this is the conference for you.

Big and small

Our conferences aren't just about spreadsheets. We curate content on broader topics, for example, advancing the art of data collaboration, from putting your data on GitHub to producing meaningful insight by running large scale distributed processing on a cluster.
